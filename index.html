About Us

The Robin Hood Army is a volunteer-based organization that works to get surplus food from restaurants to the less fortunate sections of society in cities across India and 10 other countries. Our volunteers, known as "Robins", work to reduce food wastage and serve the underprivileged.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate hunger and food wastage by connecting excess food from restaurants to people in need. We aim to build a community-driven platform where volunteers can make a tangible impact on society.

Volunteer with Us

Want to join the Robin Hood Army and help make a difference? Fill out our volunteer form to start your journey as a Robin Hood today! Volunteers are needed in various capacities including food distribution, outreach, and logistics.
