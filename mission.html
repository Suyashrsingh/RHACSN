Our Mission

The RHA's mission is to serve the hungry citizens by getting surplus food from restaurants and the community. They aim to create self-sustained chapters across the world that will look after their local community, inspiring people to give back to those who need it most.

Our Vision

Their vision is simple yet powerful: to beat global hunger and bring out the best of humanity using food as a medium. They strive to create a world where everyone has access to food, and no one sleeps hungry . In line with their mission and vision, the RHA has already made significant progress, serving over 118 million people across India and 10 other countries. They have also empowered 7,300+ street children with basic primary education through the Robin Hood Academy.
